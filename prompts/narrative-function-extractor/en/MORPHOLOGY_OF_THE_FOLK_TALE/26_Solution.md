# 26 SOLUTION

Function number: 26

Function name: 26. Solution

**THE TASK IS RESOLVED.**

(Definition: solution. Designation: N)

Forms of solution correspond exactly, of course, to the forms of tasks. Certain tasks are completed before they are set, or before the time required by the person assigning the task. Thus the hero finds out the princess' distinctive marks before he is requested to do so. Preliminary solutions of this type shall be designated by the sign *N.
